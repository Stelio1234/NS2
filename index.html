Leaks & Rumors

There has been a surge of rumors and leaks regarding the highly anticipated Nintendo Switch 2. Speculation surrounds its hardware specifications, launch titles, pricing, backward compatibility, and even potential release dates. While Nintendo has yet to confirm many details, industry insiders and leakers have provided some insight into what we might expect.

One of the most discussed aspects is the console's performance. With improvements in graphics, processing power, and storage, the Switch 2 is rumored to be a major step up from its predecessor. Below are the leaked specifications based on credible sources:

Leaked Hardware Specifications

  • CPU: Arm Cortex-A78C with eight cores – a significant upgrade from the original Switch, promising better performance and efficiency.
  • GPU: Nvidia T239 Ampere with 1534 CUDA cores, two RTX ray-tracing cores, and 48 Gen 3 Tensor cores – supporting modern graphical features like ray tracing and AI-enhanced visuals.
  • RAM: 12 GB of LPDDR5 – allowing for smoother multitasking and improved game performance.
  • Storage: 256 GB of onboard storage – a major upgrade from the original 32GB, possibly with microSD expansion.
  • Display: 1080p OLED screen – rumored to support a higher refresh rate, improving handheld gaming visuals.
  • Docking Mode: 4K output when docked, utilizing DLSS technology for enhanced upscaling.
  • Battery Life: Estimated to last between 6-10 hours depending on usage.
  • Backward Compatibility: Expected to support existing Switch games, though some reports suggest that certain titles may require patches.

While these specs are based on leaks, they suggest a powerful handheld-console hybrid that could rival previous generations of gaming consoles.

Confirmed & Rumored Games

According to leaks and official sources, the Nintendo Switch 2 will launch with an exciting lineup of games. Some of the confirmed and rumored titles include:

  • Mario Kart 9 (Confirmed) – A brand-new entry in the beloved racing series, promising fresh mechanics, tracks, and online features.
  • The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Time (Rumored) – Insiders claim a new Zelda game is in development with enhanced visuals and open-world improvements.
  • Super Mario Odyssey 2 (Rumored) – A potential sequel to the 2017 hit, featuring new kingdoms and power-ups.
  • Metroid Prime 4 (Confirmed) – A long-awaited return for Samus, rebuilt from the ground up for the new hardware.
  • Pokémon Legends: Z-A (Rumored) – A follow-up to Pokémon Legends: Arceus, focusing on the Kalos region.
  • Animal Crossing: New Frontiers (Rumored) – A next-gen installment with enhanced multiplayer features.
